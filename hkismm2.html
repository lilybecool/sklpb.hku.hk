WELCOME NOTE

On behalf of the State Key Laboratory of Pharmaceutical Biotechnology, The University of Hong Kong (SKLPB) and the Committees of the Symposium, we cordially invite you to attend “The 2nd Hong Kong International Symposium on Metabolic Medicine: From Bench to Bedside” organized by SKLPB, to be held on December 5, 2014.

The speakers of this symposium are from internationally-renowned institutes and hospitals, and will discuss several hot topics on metabolic medicine, including gut microbiota and metagenomics, immunometabolism, lipid metabolism, biomarker discovery and clinical trials. It also creates opportunity to bridge collaborations with leading scientists and clinicians in mainland China and overseas.

We look forward to your participation of this important event!

Sincerely,

Prof. Aimin Xu
Director
The State Key Laboratory of Pharmaceutical Biotechnology
The University of Hong Kong
